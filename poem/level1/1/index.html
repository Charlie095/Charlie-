I want to fly.

                                                 Flying in the sky.

                                                                         Like a bird.

                                                                                                 Full of energy!

                                                                         Full of happyness!

                                                 The lucky birds,

                         Through the trees,

                                                 to the mountains.

                                                                         See the expanse of the land.

                                                                                                 Appreciate the beauty of the nature!

                                                                        I flap my wings hardly!

                                                 Afraid to miss the most beautiful scenery.

                         The others are also flying hardly,

                                                 From mountains to rivers,

                                                                         from lands to sea!

                                                                                                 Day by day,

                                                                                                                   Year by year.
