I want to fly.

                                                                           Flying in the sky.            

                                                                  Like a bird.                                      

                                                 Full of energy!                                                   

                                Full of happyness!                                                                                 

                        The lucky birds,             

                 Through the trees,   

    to the mountains.

                                                                                                 See the expanse of the land.

                                                                           Appreciate the beauty of the nature!            

                                                                  I flap my wings hardly!                                      

                                                 The others are also flying hardly,                                                   

                                From mountains to rivers,                                                                                 

                        from lands to sea!             

                 Day by day,   

    Year by year.
